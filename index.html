About Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory

Founded with a vision to make significant contributions to cancer research, Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory has established itself as a leading institution in Newport, Kentucky. Our laboratory combines rigorous scientific investigation with comprehensive educational programs to advance the fight against cancer.

We are proud holders of the Candid Silver Medal of Transparency, demonstrating our commitment to accountability and openness in all our operations.

Undergraduate Research Education Program (UREP)

The UREP program provides undergraduate students with hands-on research experience in cancer biology, preparing them for careers in research, medicine, and healthcare.

Program Benefits

  • Direct mentorship from experienced researchers
  • Hands-on laboratory experience
  • Opportunity to contribute to published research
  • Professional development and networking
  • Preparation for graduate school and medical school
