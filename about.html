About Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory

Dedicated to advancing cancer research and education in Newport, Kentucky

Our Mission

Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory is committed to advancing cancer research through innovative scientific investigation while providing exceptional undergraduate research education opportunities. We strive to make meaningful contributions to the fight against cancer while fostering the next generation of cancer researchers and healthcare professionals.

Our laboratory serves as a bridge between cutting-edge research and practical education, ensuring that our discoveries not only advance scientific knowledge but also prepare students for successful careers in cancer research, medicine, and related fields.

Our History

Founded with a vision to make significant contributions to cancer research, Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory has established itself as a leading research institution in Newport, Kentucky. Our journey began with a commitment to excellence in both scientific discovery and education.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for rigorous research methodology, innovative approaches to cancer biology, and comprehensive educational programs that prepare the next generation of researchers and healthcare professionals.

Our laboratory has consistently maintained high standards of scientific integrity and transparency, earning recognition including the Candid Silver Medal of Transparency, which reflects our commitment to accountability in all our operations.

Located in Newport, Kentucky

Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory is proudly based in Newport, Kentucky, where we serve our local community while contributing to global cancer research efforts. Our location allows us to maintain strong community connections while pursuing world-class research and education.

Newport provides an ideal setting for our laboratory, offering a supportive community environment that values both scientific advancement and educational excellence. Our presence contributes to the local knowledge economy while advancing cancer research that benefits patients worldwide.
