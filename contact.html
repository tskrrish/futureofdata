Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply to the UREP program?

+

To apply to our Undergraduate Research Education Program, please visit our UREP page for detailed information about requirements and the application process. Applications are accepted for fall, spring, and summer terms.

How can I access your biospecimen repository?

+

Qualified investigators can request access to our biospecimen repository by contacting us with their research proposal and credentials. All requests undergo scientific review to ensure appropriate use of samples.

Do you offer tours of the laboratory?

+

We offer educational tours for academic groups and potential collaborators. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit and discuss safety protocols and availability.

How can I support your research financially?

+

You can support our research through our secure donation page. We accept one-time and recurring donations, and all contributions directly support our research programs and student education initiatives.

Do you collaborate with other institutions?

+

Yes, we actively collaborate with universities, research institutions, and healthcare organizations. We welcome partnership opportunities that advance cancer research and education.
