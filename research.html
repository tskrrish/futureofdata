Research Programs

Advancing cancer research through innovative scientific investigation and collaborative partnerships

Our Research Focus

Wood Hudson Cancer Research Laboratory conducts cutting-edge research aimed at understanding cancer biology, developing new therapeutic approaches, and improving patient outcomes. Our comprehensive research programs span multiple areas of cancer science while maintaining strong collaborative partnerships with leading institutions worldwide.

Cancer Biology Research

Investigating the fundamental mechanisms of cancer development, progression, and metastasis to identify new therapeutic targets and improve our understanding of cancer biology.

Research Areas:

  • Cellular mechanisms of cancer development
  • Tumor microenvironment interactions
  • Cancer stem cell biology
  • Molecular pathways in cancer progression
  • Drug resistance mechanisms
